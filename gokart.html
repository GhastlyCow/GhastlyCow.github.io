Overview

I decided to combine my passion for motorsport and engineering by constructing my very own go kart. I fully manufactured and welded every part of this go kart. This go kart had to be as budget friendly as possible while also being fast and fun to drive.

This go kart was created using the tools and equipment provided by NOVA Labs.
